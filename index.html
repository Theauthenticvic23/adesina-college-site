Student Opportunities

In ACI, students are closely monitored from JSS up to SSS to support their academic advancement. For this reason, the school does not admit transferred students into JSS3, SSS2, or SSS3. As a result, 92% of students who graduate annually gain direct admission into top universities in Nigeria and abroad.

JAMB and post-UTME results reflect this excellence. In the 2015 OAU post-UTME, the second-best score was earned by an ACI graduate. Similarly, in JAMB 2016, three ACI students scored over 300.

The school provides separate and fully equipped laboratories for Chemistry, Physics, Technical Drawing, ICT, and Home Economics. Additional facilities include a modern library, typing pool, and audio-visual room.

Biography:
Born June 15, 1961 in Lome, Togo, Mr. Adesina hailed from Ede North, Osun State. He co-founded Adesina College, Ibadan, where his legacy continues to shape minds and hearts.

Education:
He studied at Adogbe Primary School, Lycee De Tokoin (Togo), and the University of Ibadan.

Career:
He taught in top schools, founded ACEC in 1991, and established Adesina College in 2003 ‚Äî transforming education for over two decades.

Personal Life:
A devoted Christian, husband to Olufunke, and father of three. A teacher, mentor, and father figure to many.

In Loving Memory:
He passed peacefully on November 8, 2023. His legacy lives on in the halls and hearts of Adesina College.
